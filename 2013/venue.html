Venue

The conference will be held in Orlando, Florida at the Walt Disney World Swan Resort collocated with the IEEE Virtual Reality conference. At the heart of central Florida, Orlando is considered the mecca of simulation in America, home to both the internationally recognized National Center for Simulation (NCS) and the University of Central Florida’s Institute for Simulation and Training (IST). Hosting both commercial and military simulation companies, Orlando is rich with simulation technology innovation. Of course, Orlando is also internationally known for nearby beaches, Cape Canaveral, and theme parks such as Walt Disney World, SeaWorld, Universal Studios, and the Wet 'n Wild Water Park. These attractions, and the conference site proximity to Walt Disney World, offer a wonderful opportunity to combine a family vacation with VR.
