Contestants

The 3DUI Contest for 2013 it is over, thanks to everyone for your participation. Below you will find the results of the contest.

Winner of the 3DUI Contest

  • DIY World Builder: An Immersive Level-Editing System
    Jia Wang, Owen Leach, Robert W. Lindeman
    HIVE Lab, Worcester Polytechnic Institute

Second Prize

  • The Wonderland Builder: Using Storytelling to Guide Dream-Like Interaction
    Camille Barot, Kevin Carpentier, Marie Collet, Andrea Cuella-Martin, Vincent Lanquepin, Mathilde Muller, Esteban Pasquier, Loic Picavet, Arthur Van Ceulen, Kevin Wagrez
    Université de Technologie de Compiègne

  • MakeVR: A 3D World-Building Interface
    Jason Jerald, Paul Mlyniec, Arun Yoganandan, Amir Rubin, Dan Paullus, Simon Solotko
    NextGen Interactions, Digital ArtForms, Sixense Entertainment

Best Low-Cost, Minimally-Intrusive Solution

  • Immersive 3D Modeling with Blender and Off-the-Shelf Hardware
    Tuukka M. Takala, Meeri Mäkäräinen, Perttu Hämäläinen
    Aalto University, Department of Media Technology

People's Choice Award

  • ARWand for an Augmented World Builder
    Taejin Ha, Woontack Woo
    KAIST UVR Lab

Other Contestants

  • Building Worlds with Strokes
    David Cochard, Pierre Rahier, Sébastien Saigo, Mageri Filali Maltouf
    Silicon Studio Corp.

  • CaveCAD: Architectural Design in the CAVE
    Cathleen E. Hughes, Lelin Zhang, Jurgen P. Schulze, Eve Edelstein, Eduardo Macagno
    University of California San Diego, Calit2

  • Low-Cost 3DUI Using Hand Tracking and Controllers
    Kasper Hald
    School of Information and Communication Technology, Aalborg University

  • SculptUp: A Rapid, Immersive 3D Modeling Environment
    Kevin Ponto, Ross Tredinnick, Aaron Bartholomew, Carrie Roy, Dan Szafir, Dan Greenheck, Joe Kohlmann
    University of Wisconsin–Madison

Closing Remarks

The IEEE 3DUI 2013 has come to its end, thanks to everyone for your participation and support! Do not forget to check the best paper awards and the results of the 3DUI Contest. See you next year !
