Double-Submissions Policy

In preparing your IEEE 3DUI submission, please consider that any double publication between IEEE VR and IEEE 3DUI will not be reviewed, and will be summarily rejected. The only exception are double submissions between posters and full papers (such as a poster at IEEE VR and a full paper at IEEE 3DUI or vice versa). All other possible duplications will be checked and refused.

The work submitted to 3DUI should not be under review in another scientific conference or journal at the time of submission.
